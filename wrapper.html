Abcdef is a word-guessing game. A 6 letters word will give a 6 tiles grid. If you type an letter existing in the word, you see where it is placed. If you type a wrong letter, you see an photography representing the word to help you. If you type all the letters if the word, you win the round ! Wining, you reach upper levels, and words become harder to guess.
